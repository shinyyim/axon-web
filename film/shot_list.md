## Equipment Notes

### Camera
- Primary: ARRI ALEXA 65 (large format for maximum dynamic range in low-light void environments)
- Backup/B-cam: ARRI ALEXA Mini LF

### Lenses
- 35mm Panavision Ultra Vista anamorphic (Shots 07, 08)
- 50mm Panavision Ultra Vista anamorphic (Shots 05, 06)
- 85mm Panavision Ultra Vista anamorphic (Shots 09, 10, 11)
- 100mm macro (Shot 02 -- practical foot photography)
- Anamorphic squeeze: 1.65x (for natural oval bokeh and horizontal flare on light ring)

### Grip and Lighting
- Light ring: Custom LED ring, 3m diameter, overhead-mounted, full portal white output
- Signal Red portal: Custom LED panel, vertical, 30cm x 300cm, gelled to #E94520
- Edge/rim lights: ARRI SkyPanel S60-C, set to 5600K, hard edge with barn doors
- Overhead industrial spot (Scene 03): ARRI Orbiter with open face, hard white
- Star ceiling: Fiber optic array, cool white LEDs, variable density

### Sound
- Boom: Sennheiser MKH 8070 (ultra-directional, for isolating footsteps and material sounds)
- Ambience: Sennheiser AMBEO VR (spatial capture of flagship acoustics)
- Contact mic: Piezo on boot sole (for Terrain Boot 01 ground-contact sound)
- All sound design assembled in post. No live sound used in final mix except contact mic recordings.
