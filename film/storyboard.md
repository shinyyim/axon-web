# DUNE.X — Production Storyboard

**Title:** *"SIGNAL"*
**Duration:** 45 seconds (master cut)
**Cutdowns:** 30s, 15s
**Format:** 2.39:1 Cinemascope
**Hero Product:** Terrain Boot 01
**Voice-Over:** None. On-screen text fragments only.
**Sound:** Engineered silence. No music.
**Color Grading:** Desaturated, high-contrast. Void black + bone white + portal white. Signal red appears once.

---

## Narrative Arc

The film traces the pipeline: from the body's raw biodata signal through AI translation, into robotic manufacturing, through the flagship threshold, onto the body in motion, and finally to departure. It is not a product advertisement. It is documentation of a process that begins inside the body and ends at the edge of the known.

The Terrain Boot 01 is the artifact that emerges from this pipeline. The boot is the proof that the body's signal was heard.

**Emotional arc:** Stillness -- Pulse -- Precision -- Awe -- Gravity -- Silence

**Text sequence (on-screen):**
1. "The body generates data."
2. "Data reveals patterns."
3. "Patterns shape form."
4. "Form defines motion."
5. "DUNE.X"
6. "FUTURE YOUR PERFORMANCE."

## Scene 01: SIGNAL

**Timecode:** 0:00 -- 0:08

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Visual** | Total darkness. Two seconds of black. Then: a faint pulse of light traces the outline of a human foot, suspended mid-stride against void. Biodata streams begin to radiate from the foot -- force distribution mapped as thin luminous filaments, pressure points rendered as nodes of cold white light. The data is sparse at first, then dense, building into a wireframe architecture of the foot's movement signature. The foot is real, shot against black, with data overlaid as VFX. |
| **Camera** | Locked. Centered. Macro lens (100mm). No movement for 3 seconds, then imperceptible slow push-in over remaining 5 seconds. 48fps. |
| **Movement** | The foot completes one slow step cycle. Data streams trail the motion like phosphorescence. |
| **Lighting** | No key light. The foot is illuminated only by the data visualization itself -- cold white filaments, node points of portal white. The sole of the foot catches faint rim light from below. |
| **Sound** | Two seconds of absolute silence. Then: a low subsonic pulse, felt more than heard. 28Hz. Each biodata node emits a micro-tick -- dry, metallic, rhythmic. The ticks accumulate into a pattern. |
| **Emotion** | Origin. The body speaks first. |
| **On-screen text** | At 0:05, lower-left in Sohne Mono Light, 12pt: "The body generates data." Fades in over 800ms. Holds. |
| **Color** | Pure void black (#0A0A0C). Data filaments in portal white (#FFFFFF). Faint cold glow (#D0D8E8) at the edges. |
| **VFX** | Biodata visualization overlay -- procedural, not hand-animated. Force distribution mapping, pressure node rendering, trajectory filament trails. Practical foot photography composited with generative data layer. |

## Scene 02: TRANSLATION

**Timecode:** 0:08 -- 0:15

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Visual** | The biodata filaments from Scene 01 compress and reorganize. The wireframe foot dissolves into an abstract data field -- a parametric cloud of points and vectors rotating slowly in void. The cloud begins to resolve. Lines connect. A form emerges: the Terrain Boot 01, rendered as a computational ghost -- transparent, structural, every lattice node and midsole channel visible as data architecture. The boot rotates once, slowly, ghostlike. Then the data hardens -- the parametric cloud collapses into solid geometry. |
| **Camera** | Continuous from Scene 01 (match dissolve from foot to data cloud). Orbital camera -- slow 180-degree arc around the parametric boot form. 24fps, 4K. |
| **Movement** | Data field rotates and resolves. The boot form crystallizes from chaos into order. |
| **Lighting** | The data cloud is self-luminous -- cold white points against void. As the form solidifies, a single overhead spotlight fades in, catching the emerging boot geometry from above. Hard edge, no fill. |
| **Sound** | The biodata ticks from Scene 01 accelerate, compress, become a dense rhythmic pulse. A low mechanical hum rises -- computational processing rendered as sound. At the moment the form solidifies: silence. One beat. Then a single resonant tone, like a tuning fork struck on concrete. |
| **Emotion** | Intelligence. The machine understands. |
| **On-screen text** | At 0:10, lower-left in Sohne Mono Light: "Data reveals patterns." Replaces previous text, same fade timing. |
| **Color** | Void black. Data cloud in desaturated cold glow (#D0D8E8). Solidified boot in bone (#E8E0D4) catching hard white spotlight. |
| **VFX** | Full CG sequence -- parametric point cloud, procedural mesh resolution, generative lattice construction. Transition from data visualization to photorealistic boot geometry. |

## Scene 03: FORM

**Timecode:** 0:15 -- 0:23

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Visual** | Cut to practical footage: 6F Manufacturing floor. A robotic arm deposits material onto the Terrain Boot 01 in a dark fabrication cell. The boot is mid-construction -- the outsole and midsole lattice are complete, bone-white computational structure catching hard directional light. The upper is being printed, layer by layer. We see the 3D-printed lattice growing in real time. The camera is close enough to see individual filament deposits. Behind the fabrication cell, through a narrow window of dark glass, the faint glow of the flagship atrium is visible -- a suggestion of the space below. |
| **Camera** | Tracking shot, lateral movement left to right along the fabrication cell. 50mm anamorphic. Camera height at boot level. 60fps (subtle slow motion). Shallow depth of field -- the robotic arm racks in and out of focus. |
| **Movement** | The robotic arm moves with mechanical precision. Each deposit is exact. The boot grows. The camera passes the boot, pauses, reverses slightly -- a moment of regard. |
| **Lighting** | Single overhead industrial spotlight, hard white, focused on the fabrication zone. Everything outside the spot is deep shadow. The robotic arm catches edge light. The boot is the brightest object in the frame. Faint warm spill from the atrium glow through the window behind -- this is the only warmth in the scene. |
| **Sound** | Material placement sounds -- tiny, precise, tactile. The mechanical servo of the robotic arm: a low whir, then stop. Whir, stop. The rhythm of construction. Underneath: the low resonant tone from Scene 02 continues, barely audible. |
| **Emotion** | Craft. The machine as artisan. |
| **On-screen text** | At 0:18, lower-left: "Patterns shape form." Same typographic treatment. |
| **Color** | Industrial void. Bone-white boot under hard white light. Deep shadow (#0A0A0C). Faint warm glow through background glass -- the only hint of regolith (#C4A882). |
| **VFX** | Minimal. Practical footage enhanced with subtle data overlay on the boot surface -- faint spec readouts, structural stress indicators rendered in Sohne Mono, visible for 2 seconds then fading. Post-production grain (35mm texture). |

## Scene 05: BODY

**Timecode:** 0:30 -- 0:38

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Visual** | A figure walks. Profile shot on a vast dark stage -- the runway space of B1. The figure wears the Terrain Boot 01 with dark technical trousers, a bone-white membrane shell coat. But the camera is low, focused on the boots. We see the Terrain Boot in motion for the first time: the lattice midsole flexing, the engineered tread gripping the dark surface, the structural lines of the boot responding to the body's stride. The camera follows at boot level for 4 seconds, then rises slowly to reveal the full figure -- coat catching edge light, transparent membrane panels visible, the body in motion inside the architecture of the garment. At 0:36, the figure passes through a vertical slit of Signal Red light -- a narrow portal, 30cm wide, that the figure walks through without stopping. It is the only moment of color in the film. The red light catches the boot's surface, the coat's membrane, the figure's silhouette -- for one second, then gone. The figure continues into darkness. |
| **Camera** | Tracking alongside at walking speed. Starts at 20cm (boot level), rises to 120cm (torso level) over 4 seconds. 85mm anamorphic. 72fps (gentle slow motion). Shallow depth of field. |
| **Movement** | Deliberate stride. The coat moves with the body. The boots articulate with each step -- lattice compression visible. The red light portal crossing is a single moment, not lingered upon. |
| **Lighting** | Edge light from camera-right: hard white, catching the outline of the figure and the surface detail of the boot. The figure is a silhouette with rim definition. Background is pure void. The Signal Red portal is a practical light slit -- a vertical bar of #E94520 light, 30cm wide, that the figure passes through. It illuminates the figure for exactly 1.2 seconds. |
| **Sound** | Footsteps on hard surface. The Terrain Boot 01 has a specific sound -- engineered tread on dark stage, a precise mechanical contact. Fabric movement of the membrane coat. Human breath, barely audible. When the figure passes through the red portal: a single deep pulse, felt in the chest. Then silence returns. |
| **Emotion** | Embodiment. The data became material. The material became garment. The garment found a body. The red portal: the threshold crossed. |
| **Color** | Desaturated void. Bone-white garment with white rim light. Then: Signal Red (#E94520) for 1.2 seconds -- the only color in the entire film. Return to monochrome. |
| **VFX** | Practical. No VFX on the walking sequence. The red portal is a practical lighting rig. Post-production: subtle grain, contrast enhancement. Possible subtle biodata overlay on the boot during the first 2 seconds -- faint force distribution lines visible on the sole as it contacts the ground, linking back to Scene 01. |

## Technical Notes

### Color Grading Specification

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| **Overall** | Desaturated, high-contrast |
| **Shadows** | Crushed to true black. No lifted blacks. Void is void. |
| **Highlights** | Clean white, no bloom except on light ring (Scene 04) |
| **Midtones** | Cool, desaturated. Skin tones (if visible) pulled toward neutral. |
| **Saturation** | Global desaturation at 60%. The only saturated element is the Signal Red portal in Scene 05, which retains 100% saturation. |
| **Contrast** | High. Gamma pushed toward blacks. |
| **Grain** | Subtle 35mm film grain throughout. Finer grain in macro shots (Scenes 01-02), coarser in wide shots (Scenes 04-05). |
| **LUT Base** | Custom void grade. Reference: Kodak Vision3 500T desaturated and contrast-pushed. |

### Signal Red Usage

Signal Red (#E94520) appears exactly **once** in the 45-second film:
- Scene 05, timecode ~0:36, duration 1.2 seconds
- A vertical slit of red light that the figure passes through
- The only saturated color in the entire film
- Represents the threshold -- the moment of departure, the point of no return
- It should feel like a wound in the monochrome world, brief and surgical

### Sound Design Architecture

```
0:00  ──────── silence (true black, 2 seconds)
0:02  ░░░░░░░░ subsonic pulse (28Hz), biodata micro-ticks
0:08  ▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒ accelerating ticks → dense pulse → mechanical hum
0:14  ──────── silence (1 beat) → tuning fork tone
0:15  ░░▒░░▒░░ servo whir/stop rhythm, material placement, low resonant tone
0:23  ──────── footsteps in airlock → cathedral reverb bloom
0:26  ▒▒▒▒▒▒▒▒ sustained low hum, electrical threshold tone
0:30  ░░░░░░░░ engineered footsteps, fabric, breath
0:36  ████████ red portal pulse (single deep chest-hit)
0:37  ░░░░░░░░ return to footsteps, then fading
0:38  ──────── silence (true silence, 2 seconds)
0:40  ~~~~~~~~ sustained analog tone (220Hz), warm, resolving
0:43  ~~~~~~~~ tone decays
0:45  ──────── end
```

### Cutdown Notes

**30-second cut:** Scenes 01 + 03 + 04 + 05 + 06. Remove Scene 02 (Translation). Compress Scene 01 to 5 seconds. The pipeline is implied rather than shown: signal -- form -- threshold -- body -- departure.

**15-second cut:** Scenes 04 + 05 + 06. Boot in the light ring. Boot on the body. Boot through the red portal. Brand. The shortest version is purely physical -- the boot in space, the boot in motion, the name.

### Talent

One figure. Gender-neutral presentation. Lean, architectural build. Face never fully visible -- either in profile, silhouette, or cropped below the chin. The body is a structure, not a personality. The focus is always the garment, always the boot.

### Director's Note

This film has no music, no voice, no narrative caption. It is a process document rendered as cinema. The viewer should feel they are witnessing something being made -- not sold. The Terrain Boot 01 emerges from the body's own data, passes through intelligence and manufacturing, arrives in a sacred space, and finally meets the ground through a human stride. The red portal is the emotional peak: the threshold crossed, the departure acknowledged, and then -- silence. The brand name. The only words that matter: the manifesto fragments, appearing and disappearing like readouts on a mission screen.

Every frame should feel like documentation of departure.
