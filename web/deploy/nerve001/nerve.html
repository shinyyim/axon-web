Material System

Five materials. Every one chosen for a reason that can be stated in one sentence. No material is decorative. The construction is the aesthetic.

Sole

TPU / SLS Printed

Shore 85A-95A. 0.1mm layer height. Semi-translucent in Bone colorway — light passes through the lattice.

Upper

Computational Knit

87% polyester, 13% elastane. 6 programmed zones. Single continuous fabric with spatially varying density.

Cage

Glass-Filled Nylon

PA12-GF. Tensile strength: 80-100 MPa. The skeleton that holds everything in tension.

Membrane

TPU Film 0.4mm

Vacuum thermoformed to foot geometry. Conforms to every contour in 3-5 minutes.

Dual-Density EVA

CNC-milled from biodata profile. Soft open-cell top (25C Shore) over structural closed-cell base (45C).

Hardware

A356 alloy, T6 heat treated. 8 eyelet rings per pair. Retains casting grain — not polished.
