The scan becomes structure.

Your body generates data. DUNE.X turns that data into material. Biodata scanning is optional — but if you step onto the platform, the system reads your gait, your thermal map, your dimensions. The result is a product computed from you. No two units identical.

The body is the template. Not a size chart.

01
Gait Analysis
120-second walk on pressure-plate treadmill. Strike pattern, force distribution, cadence rhythm captured.
02
3D Body Scan
Structured-light full-body capture. 12 depth cameras. 45-second cycle on 3m circular platform.
03
Thermal Mapping
Infrared body heat distribution. Identifies insulation zones for Transit Shell adaptive layering.
04
AI Translation
Raw data becomes manufacturing parameters. Lattice density, panel cuts, membrane thickness computed in real time.
